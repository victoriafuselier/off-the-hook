About

Off the Hook is a small local business born in the heart of South Louisiana. It was founded by Kat Fuselier, a gifted artist that has been crocheting since the age of 8. Below you will find a gallery of photos showcasing beautiful, hand-crafted pieces made with love. Fill out the form and shoot Kat a message if you're interested in purchasing one of these pieces or would like to know more. Custom project requests are welcome, too! You can also shop these pieces in person at Creative Roots shop located in Lake Charles, LA.
