About

Off the Hook is a small local business born in the heart of South Louisiana. It was founded by Kat Fuselier, a gifted artist that has been crocheting since the age of 8.

Fill out the form below and shoot Kat a message if you're interested in purchasing one of these pieces or would like to know more.

You can also shop these pieces and more in-person at Creative Roots shop located in Lake Charles, LA.

Requests

Have something specific in mind? Custom project requests are welcome! Specially-made pieces are priced based off of dimensions and materials used.

Have a child who's an artist? Send in their artwork with a description of their character and watch their art come to life as a plushie!

A custom piece from Off the Hook makes a unique and thoughtful gift that is sure to be a hit with people of all ages.

If you have questions about a custom piece or would like a price estimate, don't hesitate to send a message!
